Website Re-Design

For this project web re-design I decided to redesign Walmarts website from 2001. I wanted the website to have a retro feel but keep the escense of the walmart brand from 2001. I used a star based theme throught and kept the same color pallet for the design. The pages are pretty basic not much to them which is what I wanted just simple words to click on that go with images or shapes so you know what you are clicking on unlike the website I came across. My design is friendly not just to adults, but adolecents, and old people it is simple and shows you which products you are looking at by using images not just words.
