The scope of this project is to redesign an old and outdated site in a modern light. The site that I chose to rebrand from Archive.org is BestBuy.com. For this site, I decided not to look at the current Best Buy site at all.
The purpose for doing this was to not recreate the site that they currently use, and therefore end up with nothing unique. The site that I created was derived from many ideas on the internet sourced from searched such as, "ecommerce sites", "checkout pages", "about us pages", and other searches that helped me with ideation. I decided to redesign the home page, shopping cart, product page, and about us pages. For the majority of these pages,
I just layed out the elements where I thought they should naturally go, then I took inspiration from common ecommerce sites such as Amazon and Newegg. Overall, I think that the project came out really well and I conviced
myself multiple times that it was a real site, at times finding myself clicking the "quantity" dropdown in order to change the number... They did not work in a static prototype, but my brain thought that the page was real. The
main tool that I used to create the mockup is Figma. It was not my first time on Figma, but I learned a lot through this process and enjoyed it.
