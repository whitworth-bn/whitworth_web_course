Project Description

For this project I chose to redesign the National Parks Service Website, or nps.gov. The earliest capture of this site I could find was from the 90's, meaning the old designs are about 30 years old. The goal of this project was to redesign the website, keeping the audience in mind, and creating more modern and peronalized site pages.

Below are mockups of all the pages and full sized screenshots of each page. The mockups use the size of 1920x1080 pixels while the images of the sites themselves are longer to include all information that was on the original site. Mockups are credited to freepik.com and the original capture was found on web.archive.org.
