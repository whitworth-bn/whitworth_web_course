Dark Mode Web-Design

Design

Dark mode designs have a dark background with limited color.

One of the reasons dark mode was introduced to apps and websites across the internet is its potential to reduce eye strain, especially when you’re viewing from a dark room.

Slight improved battery life. Whether you’re using a newer phone (which likely features an OLED display) or a high-end OLED TV or monitor, generally speaking, less power is required for a darker page.
