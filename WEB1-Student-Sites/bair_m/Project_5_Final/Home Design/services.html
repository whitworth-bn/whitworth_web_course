Home Design Services

Housing Concept

A selection of pre-designed floor plan templates to help you get started quickly and efficiently. Each concept includes:

  • Fixed Floor Plans
  • Aesthetic Concept
  • 3D Sketches
  • 2 to 3 design Visual Renditions
  • Minor changes can be made to better suit your needs.

This option is ideal for those who want a strong foundation with the flexibility to personalize small elements without starting from scratch.

Detailed Design

A personalized home design package created with detailed input from the client. This service is more collaborative and tailored — ideal for those who have a specific vision or requirements. It includes:

  • Custom Floor Plans (based on client-provided details)
  • 3D Sketches
  • 2 to 3 Design Variations (reflecting your preferences and feedback)

This option is well-suited for clients looking for a more individualized, function-forward design process.
